Code Clicker

Your goal in life is to write the ultimate program. Armed with nothing but a pencil and paper, you set out to accomplish this mission. Click the button or press the spacebar to write lines of code. Use lines to recruit helpers that write more lines. Upgrades for each unit unlock at 10, 25, 50, and 100 of each and click upgrades unlock after you have clicked enough times. Crank some code!
